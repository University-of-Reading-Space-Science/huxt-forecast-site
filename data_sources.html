Data Sources

Our forecasts are built upon diverse, high-quality data sources. We carefully curate and quality-control all input data to ensure the reliability of our products.

Observational Data

We integrate observations from multiple platforms including:

  • Ground-based measurement networks
  • Satellite remote sensing systems
  • Research vessel observations
  • Automated monitoring stations

Model Reanalysis

Historical reanalysis datasets provide consistent long-term records that are essential for model training and validation. We use internationally recognized reanalysis products from leading research centers.

Real-time Data Streams

Our systems ingest real-time data from operational networks to ensure forecast initialization reflects the latest conditions. Data quality control procedures are applied to all incoming observations.

Data Partners

We collaborate with national and international data providers to ensure comprehensive coverage. We acknowledge and thank our data partners for their essential contributions.

Data Quality

All data undergo rigorous quality control before being used in our forecasting systems. This includes automated checks for consistency, completeness, and physical plausibility.

Data Availability

Where possible, we provide links to the original data sources to support transparency and enable independent verification of our work.
